About Tiriji Foundation

Tiriji Foundation's living call to restore people, relationships, land, culture, and spirit through holistic community systems.

Our Story

Tiriji Foundation emerged from a deep conviction that communities flourish when leadership, healing, peacebuilding, and regenerative systems work together. Inspired by the African philosophy of Ubuntu — "I am because we are" — the foundation seeks to nurture leaders, restore dignity, and rebuild living systems in nature, culture, body, and spirit.

Our Mission

To promote cultures of regeneration and peace for every individual through sustainable systems that enhance holistic wellness and social cohesion.

Our Vision

A world where individuals and communities live in harmony with themselves, with one another, with nature, and with spirit.

Our Approach

We use a holistic systems framework grounded in Ubuntu, inter-being, connectedness, and interdependence. Our programs integrate leadership development, reconciliation, regenculture, gender equity, spiritual grounding, and youth empowerment.

Our Core Values

Ubuntu

We believe in shared humanity and collective flourishing.

Regeneration

We promote systems that restore body, spirit, land, culture, and relationships.

Leadership

We nurture leaders who transform communities.

Integrity

We operate with transparency and accountability.
