Why Volunteer With Tiriji?

Our volunteer experiences are rooted in Ubuntu — the belief that humanity flourishes through connection, service, and shared growth.

Volunteers work alongside local communities in projects focused on children support, women empowerment, regenerative farming, mentorship, leadership, and holistic transformation.

Whether you stay for two weeks or several months, your contribution becomes part of a lasting impact journey.

Community-Based Experience

Live, learn, and serve within authentic local communities while building meaningful cross-cultural relationships.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need experience?

No. Passion, adaptability, and willingness to learn are more important than formal experience.

What is included in the fee?

Accommodation, meals, orientation, local support, and project coordination are included.

Can groups apply?

Yes. We welcome schools, universities, faith groups, and organizational teams.

When can I start?

Volunteer placements are available throughout the year.
